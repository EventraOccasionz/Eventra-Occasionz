Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and 1-2 months for smaller events to ensure availability and proper planning.

Do you offer customized packages?

Yes! We understand that every event is unique. We offer fully customizable packages to fit your specific needs and budget.

What areas do you serve?

We plan weddings across India and at destinations worldwide. Contact us to discuss your location

What's included in your packages?

Each package includes different services. The Essential package covers basics, while Premium and Luxury include additional services like photography, catering, and entertainment.
